ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE
  • School of Artificial Intelligenece-Gambia (S.O.A.I) is part of a global community that aims at democrating Artificial Intelligence and Robotics to build the next generation workforce.

  • Intelligence and Robotics to build the next generation workforce.

  • The future is automated, and every job is at risk. Mitigating the risk is our commitment to action.
Problem Statement

    The corporate world has recorded an unprecedented growth in the last 15 years. Fast-foward today:

  1. Computational Power (Faster, Cheaper and More Compact).
  2. Communication (Instance and Real-time).
  3. Data and information (Ubiquitous and Exponential).
  • The above has led to a shift of industrial focus, however there is a deficit of expertise. This is because university curricula have not been widely reviewed to catch up with recent growth. For this reason, special interventions are now in high demand.

  • "65% of children entering primary school will ultimately end up working in a completely new job types that don't yet exist." -- World Economic Forum.

  • In such a rapidly evolving employment landscape, the ability to anticipate and prepare for future skills is ever icreasing to mitigate the compounding effect.

  • This is where School of Artificial Intelligence intervene to save the day.

Vision Statement:

- To build a community that: attracts talents and bring ideas to life.

Mission statement:

- We are on a journey to inspire, educate and empower 5,000 innovators and creators before the end of this decade.

Values:

  • - Inspire and Educate
  • - Data-Driven Optimism
  • - Rapid Experimentation


Objectives:

  • To nurture First Principles Thinking approach to students in solving problems or meeting challenges (Engineering ingenuity).
  • To facilitate hands-on trainings to academia and research institutions to keep up with industrial needs.
  • Bridge the gender-gap balance of this male dominant sector.
  • To mitigate the unemployment and dependency rate by leveraging innovation as a predictable driver of socio-economic growth.
  • To provide, support, encourage and facilitate robotics and artificial intelligence solutions which:
    • - Enhance health delivery and emergency supplies.
    - Enhance agriculture and farming activities.

Facilities:

  1. A Training Unit: This Unit is established in selected highly motivated schools in STEMs to host these programs, by providing intensive courses on Robotics and Artificial Intelligence designed to be highly hands-on oriented, designed in a 3-dimension tracks; Junior track, Intermediate track and Senior track.

  2. Research Hub: School of Artificial Intelligence opens its well-equipped laboratory facility at Kairaba Avenue for techies, creators, hackers, and tech enthusiasts. Tailored trainings are provided in areas of Software Engineering, Data Science, Cloud Computing, Internet of Things (I.O.T) and Blockchain. (Coming Soon).
